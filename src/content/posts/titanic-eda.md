## Stack

Python (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn), Scikit-learn (RandomForestClassifier), Google Colab

## Hasil

- **Accuracy: 82%**
- **Precision (survived): 0.89**
- **F1-score (survived): 0.84**
- **Recall (not survived): 0.85**

## Insight

Gender = predictor terkuat, age kedua. Female + younger passenger survival rate jauh lebih tinggi.
