## Problem

Stakeholder butuh insight performa cabang dan produk lintas 4 tahun data transaksi.

## Stack

Google BigQuery (data warehouse + SQL), Looker Studio (dashboard), GCP

## Hasil

- KPI: total transaksi, net sales, net profit, product count, avg branch rating
- Top 10 province by transaction & sales
- Geographic profitability map Indonesia
- Branch dengan high internal rating tapi low customer rating
- Top 5 produk paling profitable
- Trend revenue monthly/yearly + filter interaktif (province, city, branch)
